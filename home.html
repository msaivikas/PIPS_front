About Us

Prathishta Institue of Pharmaceutical Sciences (PIPS) was established in 2007, approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), New Delhi, and affiliated to JNTU, Hyderabad. The college is situated in a beautiful green campus for imparting quality education and career building.

PIPS offers pharmacy programmes such as D Pharmacy, B Pharmacy, Pharm D and M Pharmacy (Pharmaceuitcs, Pharmaceutical Analysis and Pharmacology). There are 19 Laboratories attached to various departments like Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Analysis, Pharmacology, and Pharmacognosy along with Central Instrumentation facility and Machine Room.
