D. Pharmacy

Diploma in Pharmacy is 2 years program. It is purely followed by PCI, New Delhi and SBTET of Telangana.

* D. Pharmacy sanctioned intake - 60 seats

Admission criteria

The candidate should have passed 10+2 with MPC or BiPC or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks from any recognized board of education. Seats are filled by TS DOST and B. Category admission process.




B. Pharmacy

A Bachelor of Pharmacy is a four year program with 8 semesters. It is purely followed by PCI and affiliated to JNTU, Hyderabad. B. Pharmacy degree is mandatory for registration in state pharmacy council to work as a pharmacist in India and other countries.

*B. Pharmacy sanctioned intake - 100 seats

Admission criteria

The student should have passed 10+2 with MPC or BiPC. Admission into B. Pharmacy course will be based on rank in TS EAMCET. 70% of seats filled by convenor of TS EAMCET and 50% seats are filled by management quota, based on merit of 10+2. The reservation in admission is as per the State government reservation policy.




Pharm. D

Pharm. D is six years course and it is approved by PCI. Pharm. D is aimed to give skills for pharmacy practice in India. The curriculum includes five years of theoritical and practical knowledge and one year of internship in the hospital.

*Pharm D sanctioned intake - 30 seats

Admission criteria

The student should have passed 10+2 with MPC or BiPC. Admission into B. Pharmacy course will be based on rank in TS EAMCET. 70% of seats filled by convenor of TS EAMCET and 50% seats are filled by management quota, based on merit of 10+2. The reservation in admission is as per the State government reservation policy.

Our college is having MoU with Vijayakrishna Multispeciality Hospital (VKMH, Suryapet)




M. Pharmacy

M. Pharmacy is a two years PG degree. The first year comprises of course work in two semesters. The second year is research project work submitted in the form of thesis to JNTU, Hyderabad.

PIPS offers M. Pharmacy in 3 specializations

  • M. Pharmacy ( Pharmaceuitcs ) - 15 seats
  • M. Pharmacy ( Pharma Analysis ) - 15 seats
  • M. Pharmacy ( Pharmacology ) - 15 seats
Admission criteria

The candidate must pass B. Pharmacy from a recognized university. Admission into M. Pharmacy courses will be through web counselling based on GPAT and TS PGECET. 70% seats are filled through GPAT and TS PGECET by the convenor. The rest 30% seats are filled by the management based on the merit in TS PGECET or B. Pharmacy marks/percentage.
